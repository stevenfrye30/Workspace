# Status

This is an early public-facing version of the Gods archive — a personal
attempt to set the gods of many traditions beside one another, with
family trees, a timeline, and cross-cultural archetypes.

The current goal is a stable, readable arrangement, not a finished
encyclopedia. Coverage is intentionally uneven: well-documented
traditions are heavier, smaller pantheons sit as sketches until more
reading happens. Empty patches are expected.

The project is meant to grow slowly and carefully. Figures are added
one tradition at a time and checked against sources before they are
pinned in.

Last reviewed: 2026-05-08.
