# Status

This is an early public-facing version of the Phonos study desk — a small
personal interface for phonetics, scripts, and language history.

The current goal is a stable place to return to, not a finished archive. The
IPA chart and a couple of language journals (Arabic, Telugu) are the working
core. Everything else is intentionally deferred.

The project is meant to grow slowly and carefully. Empty cells on the chart are
expected. Most pages are a single sentence today and will deepen one at a time.

Last reviewed: 2026-05-08.
