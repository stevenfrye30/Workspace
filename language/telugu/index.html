Telugu

తెలుగు — notes.
← Chart

Retroflex series: ʈ ɖ ɳ ɭ. The South Asian column. Tongue tip curls back rather than touching the alveolar ridge — Arabic has none of these.

Telugu marks long vowels with a separate sign on the syllable. ా lengthens to aː; ీ lengthens to iː; ూ lengthens to uː.
