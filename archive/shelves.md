# Deeper shelves

The shelf at the front of the archive holds a small number of texts —
the ones most often returned to. Many more entries are prepared and live
below: chapters of long works, fragments, single-verse openings. They
are kept here for those who want to walk further in.

Each entry opens at a single chapter or fragment. Multi-chapter works
are listed once; the link enters at the first chapter, and the others
follow in sequence inside the reader.
